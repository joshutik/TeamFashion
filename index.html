About Us

PMCE is an independent company that will be making things easier for you both in fashion and choices. We are committed in an inclusive company culture that respects and uplifts people of diverse back ground and point of view.

As soon as you pick your choices, we want you to be able to tell that what you have is from us. We consider every detail and ask questions like: Does the design support and enhance the content?How do you feel choosing these?What special touches can we add to make it what you'll treasure? We apply this approach to everything we make.

Based in Nigeria, our headquaters is in Port-Harcourt. There are four[4] floors; one for sales, marketing and operation, one for contracts, another for design and production and the last is a meeting space where we all come together. There's even a storefront you can visit next time you are in town.
